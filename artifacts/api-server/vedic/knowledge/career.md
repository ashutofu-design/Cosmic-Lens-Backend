# Career Astrology — Mapping Planets/Houses to Modern Professions

## Core Houses for Career Analysis

**10th House (Karma):** Primary career, profession, public-facing work, status.
**6th House (Service):** Job (vs business), daily routine, employee role, debt-handling.
**2nd House (Wealth):** Income from career, salary, savings.
**11th House (Gains):** Networking, side income, gains from profession.
**3rd House (Effort):** Marketing skills, sales, communication, hustle.

## Career Verdict — Job vs Business

### Strong indicators for JOB:
- 6th house lord strong (especially in 6/10/11)
- Mercury or Saturn dominant in 10th
- 10th lord in 6th
- No 7th lord strength (partnerships weak)
- Sun strong but in earth/water sign (steady authority, not entrepreneurial fire)

### Strong indicators for BUSINESS:
- 7th house lord strong (partnerships, customers)
- 10th lord in own/exalted sign in Kendra
- Mars/Sun strong in 10th (executive fire)
- 11th house full of benefics (multiple income streams)
- Jupiter aspecting 10th (independent advisory/consulting)
- Rahu in 10th (unconventional ventures, modern tech)

### Indicators for HYBRID (consultant/freelancer):
- 7th lord strong + 10th lord strong
- Mercury + Venus combination
- 3rd house strong (sales/marketing skills)

**Phase 1 (Saturn in 12H from Moon):** Hidden losses, foreign opportunities, restructuring.
