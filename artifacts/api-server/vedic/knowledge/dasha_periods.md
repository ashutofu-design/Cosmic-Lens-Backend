# Vimshottari Dasha — 120-Year Cycle Reference

The Vimshottari Dasha is the primary timing system in Vedic astrology. Total cycle = 120 years. Each planet has a fixed Mahadasha (MD) length, and within each MD the planets cycle as Antardasha (AD) in the same proportion.

## Antardasha Calculation

Within a Mahadasha, each planet gets an AD proportional to its own MD length:
**AD length = (planet's MD years × current MD's years) / 120**

Example: In Jupiter MD (16 years), Saturn AD lasts:
(19 × 16) / 120 = **2.53 years (≈30 months)**

## Dasha Effect — Which Lord Triggers What

### General principles:
- **MD lord = strongest theme of the period**
- **AD lord = sub-theme that activates within MD**
- **PD (Pratyantar) lord = month-precision sub-sub-period**
- A "result" requires both MD and AD to be supportive significators

### Auspicious dasha periods:
- MD/AD of Lagna lord, 5th lord, 9th lord, 10th lord, 11th lord
- MD/AD of yogakaraka planet (specific to Lagna)
- MD of strong, well-placed benefic
- MD/AD of planets in own/exalted signs

### Difficult dasha periods:
- MD/AD of 6th, 8th, 12th lords
- MD of debilitated planet
- MD of planet in dusthana (6, 8, 12)
- MD of planet aspected by malefic without benefic relief
- Maraka periods: MD/AD of 2nd lord or 7th lord (esp. for elderly)

## Sade-Sati & Dhaiya

**Sade-Sati:** Saturn's transit through 12th, 1st, and 2nd house from natal Moon. Total ~7.5 years.
- Phase 1 (Saturn in 12H from Moon): hidden losses, foreign moves, isolation.
- Phase 2 (Saturn in 1H from Moon): peak pressure, identity crisis, max challenge.
- Phase 3 (Saturn in 2H from Moon): financial-family pressure, ends with consolidation.

**Dhaiya (small Sade-Sati):** Saturn's 2.5-year transit through 4th or 8th from natal Moon.

**Modern reframe:** Sade-Sati often coincides with career pivot, sabbatical, MBA, founding a company, divorce-then-rebuild, or major spiritual awakening. The "hardship" is the stripping-away that allows authentic structure to be built.

## Dasha + Transit Combination Rule

**A prediction is high-confidence ONLY when:**
1. Dasha lord supports the event AND
2. Current transit (especially Jupiter for marriage/birth, Saturn for career/longevity) reinforces the dasha AND
3. Pratyantar (PD) lord aligns with the topic

If 2 of 3 align, prediction is "likely". If only 1 aligns, "possible but uncertain". If none align, the engine reports "no clear window — consult next significant dasha shift."

## Pratyantar Dasha (Sub-Sub-Period)

PD = sub-period within AD; gives month-level precision.
**Formula:** PD length = (planet's MD years × current AD's days) / 120

Pratyantars are critical for "next 3 months kaisa hoga" type questions. The TIMING ENGINE always cites the current PD when answering near-future questions.
