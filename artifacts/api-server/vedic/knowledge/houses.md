# 12 Houses (Bhavas) — Significations, Strengths, Modern Mapping

## 1st House (Tanu Bhava — Self)
**Body part:** Head, brain, overall physique. **Significations:** body, personality, ego, complexion, longevity, health, character.
**Strong 1H lord:** robust health, strong personality, leadership, confident appearance.
**Weak/afflicted:** chronic health issues, low confidence, identity confusion.
**Modern map:** personal brand, founder identity, "your face on LinkedIn".
**Activates in dasha:** new beginnings, identity-shift, health awareness.

## 2nd House (Dhana Bhava — Wealth & Family)
**Body part:** Face, right eye, mouth, neck, throat. **Significations:** savings, family, speech, food, jewellery, voice.
**Strong:** good family, eloquent speech, accumulated wealth, sweet voice, food/restaurant business.
**Weak:** family conflict, speech issues, savings depletion, dental problems.
**Modern map:** liquid bank balance (vs 11th = portfolio gains), family WhatsApp group, personal brand voice (podcasting, YouTube).
**Maraka:** 2nd is one of the two killer houses (with 7th); afflicted 2H lord in difficult dasha = financial/health crisis.

## 3rd House (Sahaja Bhava — Effort & Siblings)
**Body part:** Right ear, arms, shoulders, hands. **Significations:** younger siblings, courage, short journeys, communication, writing, hobbies.
**Strong:** courageous, good with siblings, skilled writer/communicator, marketer.
**Weak:** sibling-conflict, fear, weak communication, hand injuries.
**Modern map:** marketing skills, content creation, sales hustle, copywriting, podcast/blog cadence, startup grit.
**Upachaya house:** improves over time with effort.

## 4th House (Sukha Bhava — Comforts & Mother)
**Body part:** Chest, breast, lungs, heart (with 5H). **Significations:** mother, home, vehicle, land, real-estate, education (basic), peace of mind.
**Strong:** loving mother, beautiful home, vehicles, peace, agricultural/real-estate prosperity.
**Weak:** mother-issues, no own home, restlessness, heart troubles.
**Modern map:** real-estate portfolio, EV/car ownership, work-from-home productivity, mental peace, mother's blessings.
**Most powerful angle (Kendra) along with 1, 7, 10.**

## 5th House (Putra Bhava — Children & Intelligence)
**Body part:** Stomach, upper abdomen, liver. **Significations:** children, intelligence, education (higher), creativity, romance, mantra-siddhi, speculation, past-life merit (purva-punya).
**Strong:** intelligent, blessed with children, creative, lucky in speculation, devotional.
**Weak:** child-delay, learning issues, romance failure, gambling losses.
**Modern map:** product creativity, app/SaaS launches, content virality, romantic compatibility, stock-trading skill, equity gains.
**Trikona house:** highly auspicious; with 9H, gives Raja Yoga.

## 6th House (Ari Bhava — Enemies & Service)
**Body part:** Lower abdomen, intestines, kidneys. **Significations:** enemies, debts, disease, daily service-job, competition, maternal uncle, pets.
**Strong (when lord is in dusthana):** wins over enemies, debt-free, healthy, good employee/HR/legal-officer.
**Weak (when lord in good house):** chronic disease, debt accumulation, lawsuits, betrayal.
**Modern map:** corporate job (especially mid-level execution), debt management, EMI burden, court cases, gym/fitness routine.
**Upachaya house:** improves with effort and discipline.

## 7th House (Yuvati Bhava — Spouse & Partnership)
**Body part:** Lower back, kidneys (lower), reproductive organs. **Significations:** spouse, marriage, business partners, public, foreign travel, open enemies.
**Strong:** loving spouse, successful partnerships, good public image, foreign opportunities.
**Weak:** marriage delay/discord, partnership disputes, public criticism.
**Modern map:** co-founder relationship, customer-facing role, dating-app success, B2B contracts, international clients.
**Maraka house:** 7H is also a killer; afflicted lord = relationship-trauma + life-threat in late life.

## 8th House (Ayur Bhava — Longevity & Transformation)
**Body part:** Reproductive organs, anus, chronic disease. **Significations:** longevity, sudden events, accidents, occult, in-laws money, inheritance, research, secret affairs.
**Strong:** long life, deep research ability, sudden wealth (lottery/inheritance), occult mastery.
**Weak:** accidents, chronic illness, scandal, sudden losses, surgical interventions.
**Modern map:** insurance settlements, crypto/risk-investment, deep tech research (PhD, R&D), inheritance, life insurance, alternative therapies.
**Dusthana house:** generally challenging; lord here can give research depth or hidden wealth.

## 9th House (Bhagya Bhava — Fortune & Dharma)
**Body part:** Hips, thighs. **Significations:** father, guru, higher education (PhD), long journeys, religion, philosophy, luck (bhagya), legal wisdom.
**Strong:** lucky, fortunate father/guru, foreign higher-education, righteous, wins legal cases, spiritual depth.
**Weak:** father-distance, guru-issues, bad luck, legal losses, agnostic struggles.
**Modern map:** PhD/MBA from foreign university, mentor network, religious-tech (yoga apps), academic publications, legal wins.
**Most auspicious trikona; with 5H = Raja Yoga.**

## 10th House (Karma Bhava — Career & Status)
**Body part:** Knees, joints. **Significations:** career, profession, public status, government, fame, father (alternate to 9H), authority.
**Strong:** great career, public recognition, government honours, leadership position.
**Weak:** career struggles, no recognition, authority conflicts, status drops.
**Modern map:** LinkedIn profile, job title, founder-CEO role, public fame, government contracts, awards.
**Most powerful Kendra; "Karma" = visible action in the world.**

## 11th House (Labha Bhava — Gains & Network)
**Body part:** Calves, ankles, left ear. **Significations:** gains, income, elder siblings, friends, network, ambitions, fulfilment of desires.
**Strong:** abundant gains, large network, elder-sibling support, ambitions fulfilled, multiple income streams.
**Weak:** income-blockage, network-poor, sibling-conflict, unfulfilled desires.
**Modern map:** investment portfolio gains, salary increments, professional network (LinkedIn connections), affiliate income, side hustles, equity vesting.
**Upachaya + best house for income; works WITH 2H (savings) and 9H (luck).**

## 12th House (Vyaya Bhava — Loss & Liberation)
**Body part:** Feet, left eye, lymphatic system. **Significations:** losses, expenditure, foreign places, hospitals, prisons, beds (sleep + sex), moksha (liberation), charity, hidden enemies.
**Strong:** foreign success, spiritual liberation, generous charity, hospital/research-institution success, conscious dream-life.
**Weak:** losses, expenses, exile, sleep disorders, secret enemies, scandal.
**Modern map:** foreign settlement, NRI life, OTT/streaming consumption, hospital/healthcare career, meditation practice, charity foundations, secret affairs.
**Dusthana house:** difficult for material gains, excellent for spiritual + foreign + research.
