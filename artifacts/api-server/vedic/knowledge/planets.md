# 9 Grahas — Classical Significations & Modern Reframes

## Sun (Surya)
**Karaka:** Soul (Atma), father, authority, government, ego, vitality, leadership, bones, heart, right eye.
**Rules:** Leo. Exalted in Aries (10°), debilitated in Libra (10°). Friends: Moon, Mars, Jupiter. Enemies: Venus, Saturn.
**Strong Sun:** confident, principled, natural leader, government/admin/medicine career, paternal warmth, robust immunity.
**Weak Sun:** low confidence, father-issues, authority struggles, weak digestion, ego-fragility, eye/heart problems.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Sun in today's world = founder/CEO/creator/team-lead energy; same placements that gave kings their crowns now give brand-builders their visibility.
**Best houses:** 1, 5, 9, 10, 11. **Difficult:** 6, 8, 12.
**Remedies (free):** Sunday vrat, Surya Namaskar at sunrise, Aditya Hrudayam, water offering to Sun.
**Remedies (premium):** Ruby (5+ ct, gold ring, ring finger), Manik substitute red garnet.

## Moon (Chandra)
**Karaka:** Mind (Manas), mother, emotions, public, water, milk, queen, left eye, breast, fluids in body.
**Rules:** Cancer. Exalted in Taurus (3°), debilitated in Scorpio (3°). Friends: Sun, Mercury. Enemies: none. Neutral: rest.
**Strong Moon:** emotionally stable, popular, intuitive, good mother-relationship, hospitality/F&B/public-facing career.
**Weak Moon:** mood swings, anxiety, sleep issues, mother-distance, public-image instability, water-retention.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Moon = empath/therapist/UX-designer/community-builder/creator-economy native; emotional intelligence is the new IQ.
**Pakshabala:** waxing Moon (Shukla paksha) is strong, waning (Krishna) is weak. Full Moon = peak, new Moon = lowest.
**Remedies (free):** Monday vrat, drink water from silver cup, mother-seva, white clothes Mondays.
**Remedies (premium):** Pearl (Moti) 5+ ct in silver, ring finger or pinky.

## Mars (Mangal)
**Karaka:** Energy, brothers (younger), courage, land/property, real-estate, surgery, military, blood, muscles, accidents.
**Rules:** Aries + Scorpio. Exalted in Capricorn (28°), debilitated in Cancer (28°). Friends: Sun, Moon, Jupiter. Enemies: Mercury.
**Strong Mars:** courageous, athletic, decisive, real-estate/sports/defence/surgery/engineering career.
**Weak Mars:** indecisive, accident-prone, sibling-conflict, anger issues, blood disorders, low stamina.
**Manglik dosha:** Mars in 1/2/4/7/8/12 (some traditions exclude 2nd) → marriage delay/conflict; cancellable by Manglik partner OR Jupiter/Venus aspect on Mars.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Mars = entrepreneur/athlete/executor/builder; the same drive that made warriors now ships products.
**Remedies (free):** Tuesday vrat, Hanuman Chalisa, donate red lentils, exercise daily.
**Remedies (premium):** Red coral (Moonga) 5+ ct in copper/gold, ring finger.

## Mercury (Budh)
**Karaka:** Intellect, communication, business, accounting, writing, students, skin, nerves, speech.
**Rules:** Gemini + Virgo. Exalted in Virgo (15°), debilitated in Pisces (15°). Friends: Sun, Venus. Enemies: Moon.
**Strong Mercury:** sharp intellect, witty, business-minded, accounting/IT/journalism/teaching/copywriting career.
**Weak Mercury:** poor decision-making, speech issues, nervous disorders, learning difficulties, skin allergies.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Mercury = software-engineer/data-scientist/AI-engineer/copywriter/SaaS-founder; the analytical mind is today's most-paid skill.
**Budhaditya yoga:** Sun + Mercury together → strong intellect + government recognition.
**Remedies (free):** Wednesday vrat, donate green moong dal, recite Vishnu Sahasranama.
**Remedies (premium):** Emerald (Panna) 5+ ct in gold, little finger.

## Jupiter (Guru / Brihaspati)
**Karaka:** Wisdom, teacher, husband (in female chart), children, dharma, wealth, liver, fat, growth.
**Rules:** Sagittarius + Pisces. Exalted in Cancer (5°), debilitated in Capricorn (5°). Friends: Sun, Moon, Mars. Enemies: Mercury, Venus.
**Strong Jupiter:** wise, ethical, blessed with children, teacher/lawyer/finance/spiritual-guide career, good fortune.
**Weak Jupiter:** poor judgment, teacher-conflict, child-delay, liver/fat issues, ethical lapses.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Jupiter = professor/coach/therapist/financial-advisor/content-creator-with-wisdom; the guru archetype is now the thought-leader.
**Guru-Chandal yoga:** Jupiter + Rahu together → confused values, controversial wisdom, may benefit unconventional teachers.
**Remedies (free):** Thursday vrat, Vishnu Sahasranama, donate yellow items (turmeric, dal, banana), gurudev seva.
**Remedies (premium):** Yellow sapphire (Pukhraj) 5+ ct in gold, index finger.

## Venus (Shukra)
**Karaka:** Love, marriage (esp. in male chart), beauty, art, luxury, vehicles, kidney, reproductive system, semen.
**Rules:** Taurus + Libra. Exalted in Pisces (27°), debilitated in Virgo (27°). Friends: Mercury, Saturn. Enemies: Sun, Moon.
**Strong Venus:** charming, artistic, romantic, fashion/film/beauty/luxury-goods/hospitality career, happy marriage.
**Weak Venus:** relationship issues, low charm, kidney problems, lack of luxury, art-blockage.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Venus = designer/artist/creator/influencer/luxury-brand-founder; aesthetics is today's currency in Instagram economy.
**Mahalakshmi/Lakshmi yoga:** Venus + Jupiter strong + 2nd/11th lord well-placed → wealth.
**Remedies (free):** Friday vrat, donate white items (rice, milk, sugar), Lakshmi Stotra.
**Remedies (premium):** Diamond (Heera) 0.5+ ct OR white sapphire substitute, in platinum/white-gold, ring finger.

## Saturn (Shani)
**Karaka:** Karma, discipline, longevity, sorrow, masses, servants, iron, oil, old age, joints, knees, teeth.
**Rules:** Capricorn + Aquarius. Exalted in Libra (20°), debilitated in Aries (20°). Friends: Mercury, Venus. Enemies: Sun, Moon, Mars.
**Strong Saturn:** disciplined, hard-working, persistent, judiciary/government-service/mining/oil-gas/manufacturing career, long life.
**Weak Saturn:** delays, depression, joint-pain, servant-issues, slow karma, fear of failure.
**Sade-Sati:** 7.5-yr Saturn transit through 12th-1st-2nd from natal Moon → restructuring, hardships, but builds lasting foundation.
**Dhaiya:** 2.5-yr transit through 4th or 8th from natal Moon → smaller-scale Saturn challenge.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Saturn = long-game founder/operations-leader/system-builder/CFO/quant-trader; Saturn rewards 10+ year compounding effort.
**Remedies (free):** Saturday vrat, mustard oil donation, Hanuman Chalisa, serve elderly/poor, blue/black clothes Saturdays.
**Remedies (premium):** Blue sapphire (Neelam) 5+ ct in silver/iron, middle finger — TEST FIRST (only suits if Saturn is yogakaraka).

## Rahu (North Node)
**Karaka:** Obsession, foreign things, technology, deception, sudden gains, addictions, paternal grandfather, snakes.
**Always retrograde, never rules a sign permanently.** Exalted: Taurus/Gemini (debate). Debilitated: Scorpio/Sagittarius.
**Strong Rahu:** ambitious, tech-savvy, foreign-success, breakthrough-innovator, sudden wealth, social-media virality.
**Weak Rahu:** addictions, paranoia, deception, foreign-trouble, scams, mental confusion.
**Modern reframe:** Rahu = the planet of crypto, AI, viral-content, foreign-settlement, startup unicorns; if old astrology said "delusion", new world says "VC bet on the impossible".
**Kaal Sarpa dosha:** all 7 planets between Rahu-Ketu axis → unconventional life path, late but explosive success.
**Remedies (free):** Donate sesame seeds, dark blanket, Bhairav puja Saturday night, Rahu mantra "Om Rahave Namah" 18,000 times.
**Remedies (premium):** Hessonite (Gomed) 5+ ct in silver, middle finger.

## Ketu (South Node)
**Karaka:** Liberation (moksha), spirituality, past-life karma, isolation, research, occult, maternal grandfather, surgery scars.
**Always retrograde, never rules a sign permanently.** Exalted: Scorpio/Sagittarius. Debilitated: Taurus/Gemini.
**Strong Ketu:** spiritual, intuitive, researcher, occult-knowledge, detached, monk-archetype, healing-arts career.
**Weak Ketu:** isolation, depression, accidents (head/spine), confusion of purpose, blood-related disease.
**Modern reframe:** Strong Ketu = researcher/data-scientist/hacker/spiritual-teacher/longevity-biotech founder; the ability to "see what others can't" is research moat.
**Remedies (free):** Donate multi-coloured blanket, feed dogs, Ganesh puja, Ketu mantra "Om Ketave Namah" 17,000 times.
**Remedies (premium):** Cat's eye (Lehsuniya/Vaiduriya) 5+ ct in silver, middle finger.
