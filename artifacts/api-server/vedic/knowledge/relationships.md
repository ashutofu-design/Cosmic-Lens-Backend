# Relationships, Marriage & Compatibility — Classical + Modern

## Core Houses for Relationships

**7th House:** Spouse, marriage, partnerships (business + romantic).
**5th House:** Romance, dating, courtship, children.
**2nd House:** Family stability after marriage.
**11th House:** Friendships, fulfilment of relationship desires.
**12th House:** Bed pleasures, secret affairs, sleep with partner.
**8th House:** In-laws, sexual chemistry, joint finances.

## Spouse Significators (Karaka)

- **Male chart:** Venus = wife karaka. Jupiter = also analysed for wife's qualities.
- **Female chart:** Jupiter = husband karaka. Mars = also analysed for husband's energy/courage.

## Marriage Timing Indicators (works with TIMING ENGINE)

**Strong marriage windows:**
- 7th lord MD/AD activation
- Venus MD/AD (male) or Jupiter MD/AD (female)
- 2nd lord (family) + 11th lord (fulfilment) joint period
- Upapada Lagna (UL) lord MD/AD — Jaimini's strongest marriage signature
- Jupiter transit on 7th from Lagna or Moon

**Marriage delay indicators:**
- Manglik dosha unresolved
- Saturn aspect on 7th house/lord (delays but stabilises when comes)
- Rahu in 7th (unconventional partner, may delay traditional marriage)
- 7th lord retrograde (delay or remarriage)
- Venus combust (in male chart) or Jupiter combust (in female chart)

## Manglik Dosha (Mangal Dosha)

**Formation:** Mars in 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th from Lagna OR from Moon OR from Venus.
(Some traditions exclude 2nd; some insist on triple-check from all three reference points.)

**Effect:** Marriage delay, conflict in marriage, separation/divorce risk, partner-health stress.

**Cancellation rules (Manglik bhanga):**
1. Both partners are Manglik → cancels out
2. Mars in own sign (Aries/Scorpio) or exalted (Capricorn) → mild
3. Mars aspected by Jupiter or Venus → softened
4. Mars in 2nd in Gemini/Virgo → no dosha
5. Mars in 4th in Aries/Scorpio → no dosha
6. After age 28 → effect significantly reduced
7. Strong 7th lord + benefic in 7th → cancellation

## Compatibility (Ashtakoot Milan — 36-Point System)

| Koot | Points | Tests |
|---|---|---|
| Varna | 1 | Spiritual/intellectual evolution match |
| Vashya | 2 | Mutual attraction & influence |
| Tara | 3 | Health & well-being |
| Yoni | 4 | Sexual & physical compatibility |
| Graha Maitri | 5 | Mental & emotional compatibility |
| Gana | 6 | Temperament (Deva/Manushya/Rakshasa) |
| Bhakoot | 7 | Wealth, family-extension, prosperity |
| Nadi | 8 | Genetic/health, child-bearing capacity |

**Total 36; minimum 18 required for "match"; 28+ excellent.**

**Critical doshas to check separately (not in 36-point):**
- Manglik dosha alignment
- Nadi dosha (same Nadi = avoid; severe biological mismatch)
- Bhakoot dosha (especially 6-8 or 2-12 from each other's Moon)

## Modern Reframe — Compatibility in 2025+

**Classical 36-point** is still the gold standard for first-pass screening. But add:
- **Career trajectory match:** Compare 10th lords; mismatched career-energy = friction.
- **Independence indicators:** Strong Saturn/Rahu in either chart = needs personal space; classical may flag this as "incompatible" but modern relationship can thrive with this.
- **Communication style:** 3rd house comparison (modern: WhatsApp pings, direct conversation style).
- **Financial style:** 2H + 11H comparison (saver vs spender, joint vs separate accounts).
- **Lifestyle alignment:** 4th house (home), 9th (travel/values), 12th (sleep/foreign moves).

## Live-In, Late Marriage, Divorce — Modern Realities

### Late marriage indicators (after 30):
- Saturn in 7th
- 7th lord in 6/8/12
- Venus combust or in 6/8/12 (male)
- Jupiter combust or in 6/8/12 (female)
- Strong Atmakaraka in 9th/12th (spiritual delay)

**Modern reframe:** Late marriage is not "delay" — it's career-first generation; same indicator that gave "delayed" marriage at 28 in 1990 now gives focused, solid marriage at 35 in 2025.

### Divorce indicators:
- 7th lord in 6/8/12 + afflicted by malefic
- Venus or Jupiter (relevant karaka) badly afflicted
- Mars + Saturn aspect on 7th
- Multiple malefics in 7th

**Cancellation:** Strong Jupiter aspect on 7th, exalted 7th lord, or strong Upapada lord can save marriage.

### Live-in / unconventional union:
- Rahu in 7th
- 7th lord with Rahu/Ketu
- Atmakaraka with Rahu

These often reject traditional marriage; classical called this "spoiled" but modern world calls it "consciously chosen partnership without state contract."

## Children Timing & Probability

**Houses:** 5th (children), 9th (grandchildren), 11th (gain of children).
**Karaka:** Jupiter (especially in male chart) for first child.
**D7 chart (Saptamsha):** detailed children analysis — count, gender, well-being.

**Strong indicators for children:**
- 5th lord strong + 5th house unafflicted
- Jupiter in 1/5/9 or aspecting 5H
- Sub-period of 5th lord OR 9th lord OR Jupiter

**Child delay/difficulty:**
- 5th lord in 6/8/12
- Saturn or Rahu in 5th
- Afflicted Jupiter
- D7 chart shows malefic 5H

**Modern:** Many "delay" signatures now align with conscious-parenting choice (career-first, IVF, adoption). Astrology shows the karmic pattern; modern medicine + lifestyle gives interventions.
