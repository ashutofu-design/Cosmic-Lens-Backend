# Remedies — Classical Vedic Upayas

Vedic remedies (upayas / parihara) are deliberate actions to balance planetary energies. Categories range from gemstones to mantras, fasts, charity, yantras, homas, and pilgrimage. The remedies engine (`vedic/remedies/*`) prescribes specific upayas based on chart afflictions.

> RULE: Specific remedy *prescription* (which planet to strengthen, which to pacify, gem carat-weight) comes from the engine. AI may explain how a remedy works and what to do, but never invent a custom prescription.

---

## Categories of Remedy (Pillar Stages)

1. **Gentle / preventive** — daily prayer, charity, mantra (suitable for everyone).
2. **Personal practice** — fast, mala-japa, yantra, ritual bath.
3. **Material remedies** — gemstone, dhatu (metal) ring, planetary yantra.
4. **Heavy karmic** — homa, planet-temple pilgrimage, naga-pratishtha.
5. **Final relief** — Tripindi shraddha, Maha-Mrityunjaya purascharana, Sanyas/seva path.

Always start at level 1; escalate only if engine-flagged severity persists.

### Wearing protocol
1. Cleanse in raw milk + Ganga-jal + honey for several minutes.
2. Smoke with sandalwood/dhoop, chant the planet beej mantra 108×.
3. Wear at the prescribed muhurta (engine gives exact time).
4. **Carat rule**: minimum 1/12 of body weight in ratti (1 ratti ≈ 0.91 carat); precise weight is engine-prescribed.
5. **Caution**: Blue Sapphire and Diamond test-wear for 3 days; if dreams/illness arise, do not adopt.

### Forbidden combinations
- Diamond + Ruby (Venus + Sun = enemies)
- Pearl + Hessonite (Moon + Rahu)
- Yellow Sapphire + Blue Sapphire (Jupiter + Saturn)
- Emerald + Pearl (Mercury + Moon)
- Red Coral + Hessonite (Mars + Rahu)

### Universal high-power mantras
- **Maha-Mrityunjaya Mantra** (Shiva) — for any health/longevity issue. Ideal 108 daily; full *purascharana* = 1,25,000 in 40 days.
- **Gayatri Mantra** — for clarity, intellect, dharma.
- **Hanuman Chalisa** — Saturn afflictions, fear, court cases.

- **Lakshmi Ashtottara** — wealth, Friday afternoon.
- **Aditya Hridaya Stotra** — Sun/health/courage, Sunday morning.

### Mantra rules
- Use a rudraksha or tulsi mala (108 beads).
- Same time daily (morning brahma-muhurta best).
- Vegetarian, satvik food, no alcohol on japa days.
- After completing target japa, perform daśāmśa homa (1/10th in fire).

## 5. Yantras (Geometric Energy Plates)

Each planet has a yantra (mystical diagram) usually drawn as a 3×3 magic square with planet-specific number total. Energised by 108-japa of beej mantra and worshipped daily with kumkum, sandal, flower.

- **Surya Yantra** — placed in eastern wall.
- **Chandra Yantra** — north-west wall, near water.
- **Mangal Yantra** — south wall.
- **Budha Yantra** — north wall.
- **Brihaspati Yantra** — north-east, study area.
- **Shukra Yantra** — south-east, near bedroom.
- **Shani Yantra** — west wall.
- **Rahu Yantra** — south-west.
- **Ketu Yantra** — south-west / pooja room.
- **Shri Yantra** — universal Lakshmi yantra; place in pooja room facing east.

## 6. Homas (Fire Rituals)

A homa transfers strength to a planet via Vedic fire ritual. Usually done by a qualified priest. Common occasions:

- **Navagraha homa** — appeasement of all 9 planets together (annual or before major event).
- **Maha-Mrityunjaya homa** — health/longevity emergency.
- **Sudarshana homa** — protection from black magic, repeated obstacles.
- **Chandi homa** — large spiritual or family-protection event.
- **Ganapati Atharvashirsha homa** — beginning of any new venture.
- **Planet-specific homa** — Surya homa for Sun, Shani homa for Saturn etc.

## 8. Choosing the Right Remedy Stack

The engine prescribes a tier (1-5) by severity. AI should communicate the **stack** to the user as:

1. **Daily** (forever) — mantra + namasmarana + benefic-day fast.
2. **Weekly** (43 days minimum, repeat as cycles) — donation on planet's day, planet temple visit.
3. **Lifetime** — gemstone if engine-prescribed.
4. **One-time karmic clearance** — homa, pilgrimage, naga-pratishtha.

Never combine more than 3 active remedies for the same planet at once — overload disturbs the system.

## 10. Cautions

- **Never wear conflicting gemstones** (see forbidden list above).
- **Pregnancy** — avoid Blue Sapphire and Hessonite for pregnant women.
- **Mental illness** — do not start heavy mantra purascharana without guru supervision.
- **Children under 12** — gemstones generally not advised; mantras/charity preferred.
- **No remedy guarantees specific outcome** — they shift karma; effort + medical care + ethical living remain primary.
