Transits track current planetary positions against the natal chart. The engine computes precise transits; this knowledge file gives qualitative interpretation for the major cycles users ask about most.

## 1. Saturn Cycles

### Sade Sati (7½ years)
Saturn transits the 12th, 1st, and 2nd house from the natal Moon. Major karmic life-test.

- **Phase 1 — Aroh (rising) — Saturn in 12th from Moon (~2.5 yr)**: financial pressure, hidden enemies, foreign possibilities, sleep loss, expenses. Loss of close one possible.
- **Phase 2 — Madhya (peak) — Saturn in 1st from Moon (~2.5 yr)**: health concerns, personality changes, isolation, body-mind heaviness. Hardest phase. Forced maturity.
- **Phase 3 — Avaroh (descending) — Saturn in 2nd from Moon (~2.5 yr)**: family/finance friction, speech problems, detachment from accumulated wealth. End of cycle teaches what is essential.

**Returns every ~29.5 years** (so most natives experience 2–3 Sade Satis in lifetime).

**Mitigating factors**:
- Moon in own/exaltation natally → Sade Sati feels lighter.
- Saturn well-placed natally (Capricorn/Aquarius/Libra in kendra/trikona) → Sade Sati gives gains, not losses.
- Strong Jupiter natal → Sade Sati delivers wisdom and slow-built reward.

**Remedies**: Hanuman Chalisa daily, Shani Stotra Saturdays, donate iron + black sesame + mustard oil + black blanket, fast on Saturdays, never refuse food to a stranger.

### Saturn Return (Shani Janma)
Saturn returns to its natal position roughly every 29.5 years → **first return age 28-30, second age 58-60**. Triggers life-restructuring; what is built without authenticity collapses, what is real becomes permanent.

### Dhaiya / Ashtama Shani / Kantaka Shani
- **Dhaiya** — Saturn transiting 4th or 8th from Moon for 2.5 years; brings home/career upheaval (4th) or mortality reflections (8th).
- **Ashtama Shani** — specifically Saturn in 8th from Moon; major karmic-ending phase, sudden losses.
- **Kantaka Shani** — Saturn transiting 1, 4, 7, 10 from Moon; thorny phase but builds long-term structure.

## 2. Jupiter Cycle (12 years)

Jupiter transits one sign per ~13 months → completes zodiac in ~12 years. The house Jupiter transits expands and protects matters of that house.

| Jupiter transiting | Effect |
|--------------------|--------|
| 1st from Moon | Identity expansion, weight gain, optimism |
| 2nd from Moon | Wealth gain, family expansion, speech fame |
| 3rd from Moon | Effort produces less than expected; siblings issues |
| 4th from Moon | Mother/home concerns; property gain possible |
| 5th from Moon | Children, education, romance flourish |
| 6th from Moon | Health/enemy/debt relief; opponents weaken |
| 7th from Moon | Marriage, partnerships, business expansion |
| 8th from Moon | Inheritance possible; transformation, occult studies |
| 9th from Moon | Best transit — fortune, dharma, foreign, guru |
| 10th from Moon | Career promotion, recognition, leadership |
| 11th from Moon | Income jump, gains, elder sibling support |
| 12th from Moon | Expenses, foreign moves, spiritual retreat |

**Jupiter Return** at age 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, 72 — each return brings expansion in the matter of the house Jupiter occupies natally.

## 3. Rahu-Ketu Cycle (18.6 years)

Rahu and Ketu always retrograde, completing zodiac in ~18.6 years → each spends ~18 months per sign.

- **Rahu's transit house** intensifies; brings sudden gains, foreign elements, ambition, also obsession and confusion. Material breakthroughs possible if used wisely.
- **Ketu's transit house** strips away; detachment, losses, spiritual insight, accidents possible. Releases what is no longer needed.

**Rahu-Ketu Return** at age 18-19, 37, 55-56, 74. Each return is a karmic rebirthing of identity.

### Rahu's first return (18–19) coincides with major life launch (college, first job).
### Rahu's second return (~37) often brings career-peak or radical shift.
### Rahu's third return (~56) brings legacy and elder-mentor role.

## 4. Mars Cycle (~2 years)

Mars transits one sign every ~45 days (longer when retrograde). Aggressive triggering.

- **Mars in 4th from Moon** → home conflict, vehicle accidents.
- **Mars in 7th from Moon** → spouse friction, partnership flare-ups.
- **Mars in 8th from Moon** → surgery, accident risk.
- **Mars in 10th from Moon** → career conflict or sudden promotion through aggression.
- **Mars retrograde** in any house → unfinished aggressive matter resurfaces.

## 5. Mercury / Venus / Sun (fast cycles)

- **Mercury** — 23-day per sign (faster when direct, slower when retrograde 3× per year). **Mercury retrograde** = communication delays, contract revisits, travel hiccups; favourable for re-thinking, re-writing, re-uniting.
- **Venus** — 25-30 days per sign. Venus retrograde once every ~18 months brings relationship review, fashion shifts, money-recheck.
- **Sun** — exactly 1 month per sign. Annual transit through 10th from Moon = recognition month; through 6th = health-test month.

## 6. Eclipses (Grahana)

Eclipses occur when Sun/Moon transit near Rahu-Ketu axis (~2x solar + 2x lunar each year).
- **Solar eclipse** — career/identity shift; release of Sun-related matters (father, ego).
- **Lunar eclipse** — emotional/family shift; release of Moon-related matters (mother, mind).
- An eclipse falling in the user's natal Moon sign or Lagna sign → 6-month karmic shift in that area.
- **Don't start ventures within 3 days of an eclipse**. Use the period for meditation, completion, release.

## 7. Annual Transits to Watch (year-by-year)

For any user's annual reading, check:
1. Where is Saturn now? Sade Sati / Dhaiya / Kantaka active?
2. Where is Jupiter now? Best transit (9, 11, 5) or weak (6, 8, 12)?
3. Where is Rahu? Sudden-gain or obsession house?
4. Where is Ketu? Loss or moksha house?
5. Solar return chart (Tajik / Varshaphal) — separate chart cast for the moment Sun returns to natal degree, used for the entire year ahead.

The engine produces transit lists with start and end dates for each ingress; AI must quote those dates exactly, never approximate.

## 9. Transit + Dasha = Final Trigger

A natal yoga only fires when:
1. **Mahadasha lord** is willing (its house signification).
2. **Antardasha lord** is willing (refines the timing).
3. **Transit** of the dasha-lord or natal house lord activates the matter.

This three-fold convergence (Yoga + Dasha + Gochara) is the classical "trigger" — engine computes all three; AI states only what engine reports.

> Remember: A yoga without dasha-trigger sleeps. Dasha without yoga delivers nothing big. Transit without natal promise is a temporary breeze. All three together = an event.
