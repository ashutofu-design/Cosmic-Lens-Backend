# Major Yogas (Planetary Combinations) — Quick Reference

## Raja Yogas (Power & Authority)

### Classical Raja Yoga
**Formation:** Lord of a Kendra house (1, 4, 7, 10) joins or aspects lord of a Trikona house (1, 5, 9).
**Effect:** Authority, leadership, government recognition, executive power.
**Modern:** CEO/founder/director roles; ability to command teams.

### Dharma-Karmadhipati Yoga
**Formation:** 9th lord (dharma) + 10th lord (karma) conjunct or in mutual aspect.
**Effect:** Highest form of Raja Yoga; aligns purpose with profession.
**Modern:** Mission-driven founders; ESG leadership; values-led companies.

### Vipreet Raja Yoga (Reverse Raja Yoga)
**Formation:** Lord of a dusthana (6, 8, 12) placed in another dusthana.
**Effect:** Sudden rise from ashes; success after major struggle.
**Modern:** Comeback founders; bankrupt → unicorn; rags-to-riches.

## Dhana Yogas (Wealth Combinations)

### Lakshmi Yoga
**Formation:** Lord of 9th in own/exalted sign, conjunct/aspecting Venus or Jupiter; Venus strong.
**Effect:** Beauty, wealth, comforts, harmonious life.

### Maha Lakshmi Yoga
**Formation:** Strong 2nd, 5th, 9th, 11th lords + Venus/Jupiter benefic + no malefic affliction.
**Effect:** Massive accumulated wealth across lifetime.

### Kubera Yoga
**Formation:** 2nd lord + 11th lord in mutual reception or strong conjunction.
**Effect:** Liquid wealth, savings, banking success.

### Chandra-Mangala Yoga
**Formation:** Moon + Mars conjunct in any house (especially 1, 2, 5, 9, 10, 11).
**Effect:** Wealth through trading, real-estate, women-customer base.

### Vasumati Yoga
**Formation:** All natural benefics (Jupiter, Venus, Mercury, waxing Moon) in upachaya houses (3, 6, 10, 11) from Lagna.
**Effect:** Lifelong financial security; never poor.

## Negative Yogas (Daridra & Kemadruma)

### Daridra Yoga (Poverty)
**Formation:** 11th lord in 6/8/12 + 11th house afflicted by malefics + Venus/Jupiter weak.
**Effect:** Financial struggle, debt, gain-blockage.
**Cancellation (Bhanga):** Strong 9th lord, exalted benefic in 2/11, Vipreet Raja Yoga active.

### Kemadruma Yoga (Lonely Mind)
**Formation:** No planet in 2nd or 12th from Moon (Moon isolated).
**Effect:** Mental loneliness, mood instability, lack of family support.
**Cancellation:** Moon in Kendra from Lagna, Moon aspected by all benefics, Moon in own/exalted sign.

## Sanyasa Yogas (Renunciation)

### Pravrajya Yoga
**Formation:** Four or more planets in a single house, especially in 9th/12th.
**Effect:** Inclination toward renunciation, monkhood, spiritual life.
**Modern:** Quitting corporate for ashram, digital nomad spiritual seekers, monk-mode founders.

## Other Important Yogas

### Gajakesari Yoga
**Formation:** Jupiter in Kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) from Moon.
**Effect:** Fame, intelligence, wealth, respect, victory over enemies.

### Budhaditya Yoga
**Formation:** Sun + Mercury conjunct (Mercury within ~14° of Sun, not combust).
**Effect:** Sharp intellect + government recognition; sciences, mathematics, civil-services success.

### Adhi Yoga
**Formation:** Benefics (Jupiter, Venus, Mercury) in 6, 7, 8 houses from Lagna or Moon.
**Effect:** Comfortable life, supportive surroundings, executive recognition.

### Neechabhanga Raja Yoga
**Formation:** A debilitated planet gets cancelled — sign-lord of debilitation is in Kendra from Moon/Lagna, OR planet exalted in same sign is in Kendra, OR debilitated planet aspects own sign.
**Effect:** Initial struggle then massive rise; converted weakness becomes strength.

### Guru-Chandal Yoga
**Formation:** Jupiter conjunct Rahu.
**Effect:** Confused values, controversial wisdom, may give unconventional/foreign teachings.
**Cancellation:** Strong 5th/9th lord, Jupiter in own sign.

### Anagaraka Yoga
**Formation:** Mars + Rahu conjunct.
**Effect:** Anger, accidents, technological mastery (Rahu) + execution speed (Mars). Modern: hacker/red-team/military-tech.
