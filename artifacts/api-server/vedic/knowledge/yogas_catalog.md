# Yogas — Classical Combinations Catalog

A *yoga* is a specific planetary combination producing a defined effect. BPHS, Phaladeepika, Saravali and Jaimini Sutras together describe 1000+ yogas. This catalog covers the 60–80 most relevant for practical use; the engine detects them and reports presence/absence in LOCKED FACTS.

> RULE: Whether a yoga is *active* in a chart is decided by the engine, not by the AI. AI should interpret only yogas reported as PRESENT in LOCKED FACTS.

5. Nabhasa yogas (geometric pattern yogas)

8. Daridra & Arishta yogas (poverty/affliction)

## 1. Pancha Mahapurusha Yogas

A non-luminary planet (Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn) in own sign or exaltation in a kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) gives one of the five "great-person" yogas.

| Yoga       | Planet  | Native becomes |
|------------|---------|----------------|
| Ruchaka    | Mars    | Bold warrior, leader, commander, athlete |
| Bhadra     | Mercury | Eloquent scholar, businessman, diplomat |
| Hamsa      | Jupiter | Wise, righteous, religious teacher, judge |
| Malavya    | Venus   | Beautiful, cultured, luxury-blessed, artistic |
| Sasa       | Saturn  | Powerful leader, mass-influencer, slow-rise to permanent fame |

## 2. Raja Yogas

Formed when a kendra-lord and a trikona-lord conjoin, exchange, or aspect each other. Produces wealth + power + recognition.

- **Lagna-lord + 9th-lord** combination → fame and dharmic success.
- **5th-lord + 10th-lord** → leadership through intelligence.
- **4th-lord + 9th-lord** → property + fortune merge.
- **Vipareeta Raja Yoga** — when a dushtana-lord (6, 8, 12) sits in another dushtana, the negative energy cancels and produces sudden upward turn after struggle.
  - **Harsha** (6L in 6/8/12), **Sarala** (8L in 6/8/12), **Vimala** (12L in 6/8/12).
- **Neecha Bhanga Raja Yoga** — a debilitated planet whose debilitation is *cancelled* (lord of debilitation sign in kendra from Lagna or Moon, or planet of exaltation of the same sign in kendra) gives explosive late rise.

## 3. Dhana Yogas (Wealth)

- **2L + 11L** in conjunction or mutual aspect.
- **5L + 9L + 11L** combination — the "trikona of wealth".
- **Jupiter in 2nd house** strong → wealth from speech / family.
- **Venus + Jupiter in 2nd or 11th** → luxury wealth.
- **Lakshmi Yoga** — 9L in own/exalted in kendra/trikona + Venus exalted → goddess-of-wealth blessing.
- **Kubera Yoga** — Venus + Jupiter + Mercury in 2nd house → treasury wealth.
- **Indra Yoga** — 5L in 11th + 11L in 5th → king-like prosperity.
- **Mahabhagya Yoga** — male: born in day, Sun-Moon-Lagna in odd signs; female: born at night, all three in even signs → great fortune from birth.

## 4. Specialised Greatness

- **Saraswati Yoga** — Mercury + Venus + Jupiter in kendra/2/5/9 with Jupiter strong → great scholarship, eloquence, wisdom.
- **Brahma Yoga** — Jupiter, Venus, Mercury in 4/9/11 from Lagna or Moon → spiritual + worldly success, philosopher-king.
- **Budha-Aditya Yoga** — Sun + Mercury within close orb (most often in 10th/11th) → fame in intellect, government, leadership in writing.
- **Gajakesari Yoga** — Jupiter in kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) from Moon → wisdom, fame, lasting reputation, lion-elephant-strong personality.
- **Adhi Yoga** — benefics (Mercury, Venus, Jupiter) in 6th, 7th, 8th from Moon → stability, ministerial-rank.
- **Pushkala Yoga** — Moon-lord with Moon, both in kendra, aspected by Lagna-lord → speech-fame, royal favour.

## 5. Nabhasa Yogas (geometric patterns)

Planets distributed in specific geometric arrangements:

- **Rajju** — all planets in chara (movable) signs → traveller, restless.
- **Musala** — all planets in sthira (fixed) signs → wealthy, steady, possessive.
- **Nala** — all planets in dvi-svabhava (dual) signs → versatile, business-minded.
- **Mala** — three benefics in three kendras → garland of fortunes.
- **Sarpa** — three malefics in three kendras → snake-like cunning, struggle.
- **Gada** — all planets in two consecutive kendras → mace; aggressive achiever.
- **Shakata** — all planets in 1st & 7th → cart-like, ups-and-downs.
- **Pakshi** — all planets in 4th & 10th → bird-like, traveller.
- **Sringataka** — all planets in trikona (1, 5, 9) → mountain-peak, great achievement.
- **Hala** — all planets in three trikonas (1-5-9 or 2-6-10 or 3-7-11 or 4-8-12) → plow yoga, agricultural / land prosperity.
- **Veena** — all 7 planets in 7 different signs → musical, harmonious genius.

## 6. Solar / Lunar Yogas

- **Vesi Yoga** — planet (not Moon) in 2nd from Sun → benefic = good fame, malefic = troubled.
- **Vasi Yoga** — planet in 12th from Sun.
- **Ubhayachari Yoga** — planets on both sides of Sun → balance.
- **Anapha Yoga** — planet (not Sun) in 12th from Moon → influences personality.
- **Sunapha Yoga** — planet in 2nd from Moon → wealth.
- **Durudhara Yoga** — planets on both sides of Moon → courageous protector.
- **Kemadruma Yoga** — Moon ALONE (no planet in 2nd or 12th from Moon, and no planet in kendra from Lagna) → poverty, mental loneliness, struggle without family support. Cancelled if Moon is exalted, full, in kendra, or aspected by Jupiter.

---

## 7. Sanyasa & Pravrajya Yogas (renunciation)

Four or more planets (excluding Moon) in one sign indicate strong renunciation tendency. Specifically:

- **Pravrajya Yoga** — 4+ planets in one sign aspected by Saturn or Mars → leaves home for spiritual life.
- **Atma-karaka in Pisces or 12th house** → moksha-oriented soul.
- **Saturn + Moon in 12th** → philosophical detachment.
- **Ketu in 9th** strong → spiritual lineage, prone to renounce worldly success.

---

## 8. Daridra & Arishta Yogas (poverty / affliction)

- **Daridra Yoga** — 11L in 6/8/12, or 11L combust → blocks income.
- **Kemadruma Yoga** (above) → poverty + isolation.
- **Anti-Saraswati** — Mercury debilitated + Jupiter weak + 5L afflicted → poor learning.
- **Balarishta Yogas** — afflictions to Moon in childhood (most operate up to age 8); cancelled by strong Jupiter aspect.
- **Madhyarishta Yogas** — afflictions to longevity in middle age (operate 8–32).

## 9. Special / Most-Asked Yogas

### Kalasarpa Yoga
All 7 planets between Rahu-Ketu axis. 12 types depending on which house Rahu sits in.
- **Effect**: delayed but eventual karmic success; struggle till one's planetary "season"; foreign elements often involved; hidden talents.
- **Cancelled by**: any planet OUTSIDE the Rahu-Ketu arc, or Jupiter aspect on Rahu.
- **Remedies**: Naga-Pratishtha, Rahu-Ketu shanti at Trimbakeshwar/Kalahasti, daily Maha-Mrityunjaya jaap.

### Mangal Dosha (Kuja Dosha)
Mars in 1, 4, 7, 8, 12 from Lagna, Moon, or Venus.
- **Marriage delay**, conflict, possible widowhood/violence.
- **Cancellation rules**: Mars in own/exalted sign, Mars aspected by Jupiter, both partners have Mangal Dosha (mutual cancellation), Mars in Cancer/Leo/Aquarius/Pisces (mild), Mars exalted in Capricorn (auspicious).
- Some lineages count from Moon and Venus too — engine considers all three.

### Sade Sati
Saturn transiting 12th, 1st, and 2nd from natal Moon (~7.5 years).
- Universal life-test phase. Three sub-phases: Aroh (rising), Madhya (peak), Avaroh (descending). Madhya is hardest.
- Returns roughly every 30 years.
- Remedies: Hanuman Chalisa daily, donate iron + sesame on Saturdays, fast on Saturdays, Shani temple visits.

### Gandanta Yoga
Moon at junction of Water-Fire signs (Cancer/Leo, Scorpio/Sagittarius, Pisces/Aries) within last/first nakshatra-pada.
- Karmic knot for self/family member depending on placement.

### Putra Dosha
5th house, 5L, Jupiter all weak/afflicted → child-bearing difficulty. Remedy: Santan-Gopal mantra, fast on Thursdays, donate to children's charity.

### Pitra Dosha (vs Pitra Rin in Lal Kitab)
Sun + Rahu in 9th, or 9L in 6/8/12 → ancestral karma blocking dharma.
- Remedy: Tarpan to ancestors monthly Amavasya, Tripindi shraddha at Trimbakeshwar/Gaya.

### Guru Chandal Yoga
Jupiter conjunct Rahu → spiritual confusion, false guru tendencies, distorted wisdom. Remedy: Guru mantra (Brihaspati), donate yellow items Thursdays, avoid taking spiritual instruction from charismatic strangers.

### Vish Yoga (Poison)
Saturn + Moon conjunct → mental heaviness, depression, slow energy. Remedy: Maha-Mrityunjaya, Moon worship Mondays, white-cloth donation.

### Anga-Hina Yoga
Moon + Saturn in 6/8/12 → physical limb weakness or chronic injury. Cross-check medical-astrology engine.

### Kala-Yoga / Kalanidhi Yoga
Jupiter in 2nd or 5th in Cancer/Sagittarius/Pisces with Mercury+Venus aspect → great wealth + scholarship.

## Yogabhanga (Yoga-cancellation) Rules

A yoga that is "present" by combination is *cancelled* if:
1. The yoga-forming planet is debilitated and not subject to neecha-bhanga.
2. The yoga-forming planet is combust (within 6° of Sun, except Mercury at 3°).
3. The yoga-forming planet is in a trika house (6, 8, 12) without Vipareeta-rule application.
4. The yoga-forming planet is aspected by 3+ malefics without benefic counterbalance.
5. The Lagna lord is severely afflicted — any chart-strength is reduced.

Engine reports **gross presence**; AI must check **strength** before promising results.

## How AI should describe a yoga

When LOCKED FACTS reports yoga = PRESENT:
1. State the yoga name and forming planets.
2. State the classical effect (from this catalog).
3. Note strength: planet's dignity, house, aspects (engine provides this).
4. If yoga is partially cancelled, qualify the result.
5. Connect to user's specific question (career/marriage/wealth) using the relevant aspect.
6. **Never quote a yoga the engine did not report as present.**
